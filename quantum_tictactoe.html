QUANTUM MOVES. Each turn you place TWO ghost marks of your symbol in two different cells. They represent a quantum superposition: your mark exists in either cell, but neither has "become real" yet.

TEMPORAL DECAY. Every 3rd turn, one random live ghost-pair vanishes from the board. Plan around the entropy.

GRAVITY ZONES. One cell glows each round as the gravity well. If a collapsing edge has the gravity cell as an endpoint, the real mark is pulled there.

WINNING. Be first to get 3 real (collapsed) marks in a row, column, or diagonal.
