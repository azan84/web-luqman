ABOUT

a small tale of forgetting

This is a short journey of maybe twenty minutes. You have no hit points. You have only memories, and each of them is both your strength and a piece of who you were.

Every spell, every word, every blow — paid in remembrance. Spend the wrong ones, and you survive as someone else.

Controls: Arrow keys or WASD to move. Space or Enter to interact with anything adjacent. E opens settings.
